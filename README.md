## 1. Introduction / Overview
The **Online Jewellery Shop** is a web-based platform designed to provide customers with an easy, secure, and convenient way to browse, select, and purchase jewellery online.  

The system allows users to:
- View jewellery by category  
- Check product details  
- Compare prices  
- Make purchases through a secure payment gateway  

**Admins** will manage product listings, inventory, and customer orders.

---

## 2. Problem Statement
In traditional jewellery businesses, customers must visit physical stores, which can be time-consuming and inconvenient.  
Moreover, limited access to variety and price comparison affects customer satisfaction.  

Therefore, there is a need for an **online jewellery platform** that offers a wide range of designs and easy purchase options from anywhere.

---

## 3. Objectives
- To design and develop an interactive online jewellery shopping website.  
- To provide user-friendly navigation for browsing jewellery collections.  
- To enable secure online payments and order management.  
- To allow administrators to manage products, categories, and stock efficiently.  
- To improve customer convenience and business reach through digital sales.

---

## 4. Scope of the Project
**Users:**  
- Customers  
- Admin  

**Modules:**
- Customer Registration & Login  
- Product Browsing & Search  
- Shopping Cart & Checkout  
- Online Payment System (e.g., EasyPaisa, JazzCash, PayPal)  
- Admin Panel for Product and Order Management  

---

## 5. Tools and Technologies
- **WordPress**  
- **WooCommerce**  
- **Elementor** for frontend customization  
- **MySQL** for data storage  
- **Stripe / PayPal** for payment processing  

---

## 6. Expected Outcome
A fully functional **Online Jewellery Shop** website that allows customers to purchase jewellery items securely online, helping jewellery businesses expand their reach and increase sales digitally.
